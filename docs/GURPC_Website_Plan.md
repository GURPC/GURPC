# GURPC Website Project Plan

## 1. Website Purpose & Goals
**Primary goal:** Provide a central, accessible hub for research collaboration, knowledge sharing, skill-building, and showcasing achievements within Green University of Bangladesh.

**Key objectives:**
*   Encourage early research participation among students.
*   Facilitate idea brainstorming, team formation, and project collaboration.
*   Clear misconceptions about research and make it feel approachable and enjoyable.
*   Highlight GURPC activities, events, resources, and member publications.
*   Build a supportive network of students, alumni, and faculty.

## 2. Target Audience
*   Current GUB students (all departments)
*   GUB alumni (especially those in research, academia, industry R&D)
*   GUB faculty members
*   Potential external collaborators (optional future phase)

## 3. Functional Requirements (Core Features)

### Core / Must-have features
*   **Home Page:** Welcoming message, mission, news, announcements, quick links, featured projects.
*   **User Authentication & Profiles:** Registration (GUB email verify), Roles (Student, Alumni, Faculty, Admin), Editable profiles (research interests, skills, links).
*   **Idea Board / Brainstorming Space:** Post ideas, comment/upvote, "Looking for teammates" filter.
*   **Research Projects Section:** List ongoing/completed projects, project pages (team, progress, deliverables), invite members.
*   **Publications Showcase:** Gallery/list of publications (DOI, PDF), filters, member submission.
*   **Events & Calendar:** Workshops, seminars, hackathons, registration/RSVP, past event archives.
*   **Resources & Learning Section:** Guides ("How to start", "LaTeX"), curated tools/courses.
*   **Discussion Forum / Community Feed:** Topic-based threads, Q&A, feedback.
*   **Admin Dashboard:** Moderation (posts/projects/events/users/content), Analytics.

### Nice-to-have / Future phases
*   Mentorship matching
*   Newsletter subscription
*   Google Scholar/ORCID integration
*   Mobile app
*   Blog section

## 4. Non-Functional Requirements
*   **Usability:** Mobile-responsive, simple navigation, accessible (WCAG).
*   **Performance:** Fast loading (< 3s), optimized media.
*   **Security:** HTTPS, secure auth, spam protection, data protection compliance.
*   **Scalability & Maintainability:** Handle 500-2000 users, easy content updates, modular code.
*   **Reliability:** Backups, high uptime.
*   **SEO:** Friendly URLs, meta tags.

## 5. Suggested Technology Stack
*   **Recommended (Beginner-friendly):** HTML/CSS/JS + PHP + MySQL.
*   **Modern & Scalable:** React/Next.js + Node/Express/Firebase + MongoDB/Postgres.
*   **Quick Start:** WordPress + Elementor + Plugins.

## 6. Development Roadmap
1.  **Planning & Design (1–2 weeks):** Finalize features, wireframes, tech stack.
2.  **Development (4–10 weeks):** Core pages, auth, main features, testing.
3.  **Content Population:** Initial data, invite members.
4.  **Launch & Promotion:** Soft launch, announcements.
5.  **Maintenance & Iteration:** Feedback, new features.
